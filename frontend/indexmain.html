Unaccompanied Minors Lounge

Help Through The Airport

Our dedicated staff provides comprehensive assistance from check-in to boarding, ensuring a smooth and stress-free airport experience for young travelers.

Priority Boarding

Unaccompanied minors receive priority boarding, allowing them to settle comfortably into their seats before other passengers, reducing any pre-flight anxieties.

Care On The Flight

During the flight, our cabin crew keeps a watchful eye on unaccompanied minors, providing attentive care, meals, and entertainment to make their journey enjoyable.

Support When They Land

Upon arrival, our ground staff will meet and assist unaccompanied minors through customs and baggage claim, ensuring they are safely handed over to their designated guardian.
